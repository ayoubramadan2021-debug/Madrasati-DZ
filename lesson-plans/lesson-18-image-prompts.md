# Lesson 18 image prompts — أُعَيِّنُ المَوْقِعَ فِي الفَضَاءِ

## قواعد الصور
- الأسلوب: 4K educational cartoon, Algerian primary classroom, warm colors, clean simple layout.
- لا شعارات.
- لا نصوص داخل الصورة إلا إذا طلبنا لوحة كلمات.
- الصور غير مزدحمة.
- كل صورة يجب أن توضّح علاقة مكانية واحدة فقط.
- نفس المعلمة في كل الصور: teacher Taline, kind Algerian female teacher wearing a modest pink hijab and long dress.
- الأطفال بعمر 6 سنوات.
- نسبة الصورة: 4:3 أو 1:1، مناسبة للعرض داخل بطاقة الدرس.
- اجعل العناصر كبيرة وواضحة.

---

## s1.webp — المقدمة

Prompt:
Create a 4K educational cartoon image for Algerian first-grade children. A kind Algerian female teacher named Taline, wearing a modest pink hijab and long dress, stands in a bright classroom with three happy children. Around them there are clear objects: a table, a chair, a school bag, and a ball. The teacher points gently to the objects as if teaching positions in space. Clean simple composition, warm colors, no text, no logos, child-friendly, high quality.

Purpose:
مقدمة الدرس: أين يوجد الشيء؟

Arabic lesson text:
مَرْحَبًا أَطْفَالِي. اليَوْمَ سَنَتَعَلَّمُ كَيْفَ نُحَدِّدُ مَكَانَ الشَّيْءِ.

---

## s2.webp — يمين ويسار

Prompt:
Create a 4K educational cartoon image. A young Algerian boy stands in the center facing the viewer. On the boy's right side there is a cute cat. On the boy's left side there is a cute dog. Make right and left positions very clear and not confusing. The background is a simple classroom. Teacher Taline is nearby pointing gently, but the boy, cat, and dog must be the main focus. No text, no logos, clean layout, child-friendly.

Purpose:
يمين / يسار

Arabic lesson text:
أَنْظُرُ إِلَى الطِّفْلِ. القِطُّ عَنْ يَمِينِهِ، وَالكَلْبُ عَنْ يَسَارِهِ.

---

## s3.webp — فوق وتحت

Prompt:
Create a 4K educational cartoon image. In a simple classroom, show one clear table in the center. A colorful book is placed above/on top of the table. A ball is under the table. Teacher Taline points to the table. Keep the image simple and clear, no extra distracting objects, no text, no logos.

Purpose:
فوق / تحت

Arabic lesson text:
أَنْظُرُ إِلَى الطَّاوِلَةِ. الكِتَابُ فَوْقَ الطَّاوِلَةِ، وَالكُرَةُ تَحْتَ الطَّاوِلَةِ.

---

## s4.webp — أمام وخلف

Prompt:
Create a 4K educational cartoon image. Show one clear classroom chair in the center. A young Algerian child stands in front of the chair, clearly visible. A school bag is behind the chair, partly visible but clear. Teacher Taline points to the chair. Make front and behind positions very obvious. No text, no logos, clean child-friendly design.

Purpose:
أمام / خلف

Arabic lesson text:
أَنْظُرُ إِلَى الكُرْسِيِّ. الطِّفْلُ أَمَامَ الكُرْسِيِّ، وَالحَقِيبَةُ خَلْفَ الكُرْسِيِّ.

---

## s5.webp — داخل وخارج

Prompt:
Create a 4K educational cartoon image. Show a large open box in the center of a classroom. A ball is clearly inside the box. A small doll is clearly outside the box, next to it. Teacher Taline points to the box. Keep the scene simple and very clear for a 6-year-old child. No text, no logos.

Purpose:
داخل / خارج

Arabic lesson text:
أَنْظُرُ إِلَى الصُّنْدُوقِ. الكُرَةُ دَاخِلَ الصُّنْدُوقِ، وَالدُّمْيَةُ خَارِجَ الصُّنْدُوقِ.

---

## s6.webp — الخلاصة

Prompt:
Create a 4K educational cartoon image. Teacher Taline stands beside a clean classroom board showing simple visual icons only, not Arabic text: left arrow, right arrow, object above table, object under table, child in front of chair, bag behind chair, ball inside box, toy outside box. Three happy children look at the board. Clean summary scene, no written words, no logos, child-friendly.

Purpose:
خلاصة الكلمات المكانية

Arabic lesson text:
أَحْسَنْتُمْ. تَعَلَّمْنَا أَنْ نُحَدِّدَ المَكَانَ بِكَلِمَاتٍ مُنَاسِبَةٍ.
